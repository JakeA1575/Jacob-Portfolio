Custom Cursor — The kind of detail that makes people notice

That glowing dot tracking your mouse is something I added — a small touch that immediately signals a site was made with intention. The ring lags behind using a lerp smoothing loop. It's the kind of detail clients don't ask for but love when they see it.

02
CSS Animation

Scroll Animations — Sections that come to life

Every section on this page slides in as you scroll. I use a vanilla IntersectionObserver to watch for elements entering the viewport, then trigger CSS transitions — no libraries, no bloat.

03

Marquee Strips — Smooth, looping content bands

The scrolling ticker above is pure CSS — a duplicated flex row animated with a single keyframe. It works at any screen size and costs zero JavaScript. Great for services, tags, or brand names.

How I Work

My process

01
You Tell Me What You Need

We have a quick conversation about your site — who it's for, what it needs to do, what you like the look of. I ask questions, you give me direction. No forms, no briefs.

02
I Design and Build It

I write the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript by hand. Every layout, animation, and interaction is custom — no drag-and-drop, no page builders, no themes you've seen before.

03
You Review and We Refine

You see it early and give feedback. I adjust until it's right. I'd rather get notes than hand over something that doesn't fit what you had in mind.

04
It Goes Live

I help get the site hosted and make sure it works across devices. You get a clean, fast, fully custom site — and I'm available if you need anything changed.
